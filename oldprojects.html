Here are some of the other projects that I've completed during my academic years.

Nebulicious

Welcome to Nebulicious, your one-stop shop for all donuts in the galaxy. You are the sole chef and must beam up ingredients to prepare donuts for your hungry customers. Don't be late with their orders. I took part in Society of Play's Hexcode game jam in a team of 6 as a Technical Artist.

  • Used: Unity and Blender
  • Created Shaders using Shader Graph
  • Created Visual Effects using Visual Effects Graph

Microcosm

Microcosm is a short game where the player goes through a set of daily tasks which will later turn into the player needing to find their medication as effects of their schizophrenia start to occur. I took part in Aesthetician Lab's 2 day game jam, where the theme was "Free Him". I thought of the idea to focus on a more mental approach rather than trying to escape captivity. I worked with one other person where we collaborated in designing the game and how the player will progress throughout it.

  • Used: Unity and Blender
  • Created a basic factured mirror model
  • Created Visual Effects and basic shaders

Doodle Day

This game was made for a 24-hour game jam hosted by Wolfjaw studios. I was a part of a 5 person team and was the technical artist for the game. We used Unity as our engine used its Universal Render Pipeline.

  • Created the CRT shader used to give the game a more retro feeling
  • Created a randomized planet generation shader to create multiple different patterns for each planet
  • Made animations for the boot up, screen shake, and the BONK sprite
